WhatWe Think

Our vision

Speak Louder wants to be recognized as a leading and consolidated organization in environmental matters, committed to improving the quality of life of the population and the sustainable management of natural resources, in a world where people live in equality and dignity and in harmony with nature. To do this, it makes available the best professional capacities to guarantee high levels of efficiency in its actions while at the same time acquiring a strong commitment with all its stakeholders to fulfill its mission.

Our Mission

The mission, of Speak Louder is to contribute to the conservation and sustainable management of natural resources and the environment from justice and solidarity, participating in the execution and administratio n of strategic environmental development projects at the local, national and international levels. These are the general guidelines that should govern our day-to-day actions within the Speak Louder, it is the duty of each of us to internalize them and align our efforts towards the achievement of the vision.

Our Values

Responsibility
At all times we are aware of the responsibility towards the world in which we live. We are aware of the repercussion of the project and that we are going to cause a philosophical overturn necessary for the birth of a metaverse free of prejudices where everyone has a place, where color, politics, or the way of seeing the world are not impediment to establish personal relationships

Honesty
We will always be clear and transparent, this is intrinsic to our DNA, and shows who we are. We promise to help all groups in their visibility, we also promise to dedicate ourselves exclusively to our NFT owners, to ensure that each one of you can express yourself freely in the metaverse, with rules made for us and by us.

Ferocious Growth
With our motto “Keep Climbing”, it is clear that our appetite for growth is insatiable. Needed this mentality to build this new world parallel to the one that exists. In fact, without this, nothing worthy has ever been achieved. We believe that there is tremendous value to conquer in the world, when we conquer it, we will learn new rules that will allow us all to be happier. We will grow steadily and fiercely. Our vision of a free world will spread like a renewing and liberating fire

Adaptability
The interoperability and scalability of the project will touch the doors and hearts of the whole world. We hope to be compatible with the VR world of the future. We have all seen that the world of smartphones does not make us happy, it does not give us everything we want, what is more, it increasingly confronts us with each other. We are not our enemy, in a future where there will be more and more room for thought due to technological development, we have to lay the foundations for a happy society.
